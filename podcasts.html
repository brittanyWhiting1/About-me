My favorite podcasts

Whether it's a long road trip or my drive to school you will catch me listening to podcasts, mostly true crime!

Cult Liter: My go to podcast. Host Spencer Henry dives into stories of cults, crimes, televangelists, and much much more. He is great at what he does and I look foward to hear a new episode each week.

Mile Higher Podcast: Hosts Kendall Rae and Husband Josh Thomas, focuses on True Crime but they delve into many other topics including conspiracy theories, unexplained phenomena, metaphysics, futurism, ancient civilizations and news stories the mainstream media doesn't cover. Another podcast I can't get enough of.
